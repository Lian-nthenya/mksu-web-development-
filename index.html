Avocado $ Chickpie Salad

A delicious and nutritious salad loaded with fresh ingredients

Ingredients;

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the avocado, tomatoes and red onion.
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle olive oiland lemon juice then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Sprinkle feta cheese on top and toss gently.
  5. Enjoy your nutritions salad!
